About RSD Direct

RSD Direct is a platform that connects suppliers and resellers focused on made-in-USA and domestic supply — built by Real Space Digital to monetize American manufacturing through media-first, avatar-powered commerce.

Entity Definition

What RSD Direct Is — Plainly Stated

RSD Direct is a platform that provides access to verified, made-in-USA suppliers and authorized reseller opportunities.

We are not a marketplace. We are not a drop-shipping aggregator. We are a curated, media-powered reseller network — where every supplier is vetted, every margin is MAP-protected, and every reseller is positioned to win through content and brand education, not just price competition.

The differentiator is the media stack. Genie Vee™ — RSD Direct's AI avatar product educator — lives on every product detail page in the network. No competing reseller has this. It is the reason RSD Direct resellers close at higher rates and earn repeat customers while others compete on price alone.

"We built RSD Direct because the opportunity was sitting right on top of infrastructure Real Space Digital already owned. Avatar production. Automation. Media creation. The only missing piece was the reseller network — so we built it."

The 90-Day Commitment

One flagship wellness store. 3–5 dealer approvals. First $500+ order within 120 days. $5,000 budget cap. A single go/no-go decision at Day 90.

Operating Principles

How We Operate

01
USA-Made First

Every product is verified for domestic provenance before entering the network. No exceptions. No grey-market sourcing.

02
MAP Always

Minimum Advertised Price policies are non-negotiable. We protect our suppliers' pricing integrity and our resellers' margin on every transaction.

03
Media Over Price

We compete on buyer education and brand experience — not discounting. Genie Vee™ is our weapon. Content is our moat.

04
Zero Inventory

No reseller in the RSD Direct network touches inventory. Suppliers ship direct. Capital stays in growth, not in warehouses.
