Why RSD Direct

The Case for USA-Made Authorized Dealer Programs

MAP Protection = Margin Protection

USA-made manufacturers enforce Minimum Advertised Price policies to protect their dealer network from race-to-the-bottom pricing. As an authorized reseller, your 25–40% margin is contractually protected — no competing with offshore dumpers on price.

Drop-Ship = Zero Inventory Risk

Every supplier in the RSD Direct network ships directly to your end customer. You never touch the product. No warehouse. No capital tied up in inventory. Your margin flows from order confirmation to your account, period.

Domestic Supply = Delivery Certainty

USA-made and USA-stocked products mean predictable lead times, no customs delays, no tariff exposure, and domestic warranty fulfillment — all of which your customer will pay a premium for.
